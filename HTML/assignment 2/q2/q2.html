Welcome To GLA University

In 1991, Shri Narayan Das Agrawal decided to fulfill his father's dream, the Late Shri Ganeshi Lal Agrawal, of establishing an institute for quality education for the people and the region & beyond. And initiated Sri Jagannath Prasad Ganeshi Lal Bazaz Charitable Trust Samiti to make the holy city Mathura a recognized destination for knowledge seekers from different spheres of life. This led to the foundation of a milestone at the karmabhoomi of the versatile and sagacious Lord Krishna.

Mathura is a holy city in Uttar Pradesh, Northern India. It is famous as the birthplace of the lord krishna and is on the banks of the Yamuna river. Mathura attracts thousands of Hindu devotees the year round all the holy places in the region. The many sites and temples dedicated to Krishna are usually the major spots of attraction. Most of the pilgrims come to Mathura during Janmashthami which is considered to be the birth date of Krishna. Mathura is quite well connected with the rest of the country. There are two domestic airports - the Kheria airport, Agra (49 km) and the Deen Dayal Upadhyay Airport, Agra (62.8 km) and one international airport - IGI Delhi (169 kms) around Mathura. From there taxis, cabs and other modes of transport re readily available the year round. Known for its hospitality, visitors can always rely to find the most budget friendly 4 star hotels in mathura.
